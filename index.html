About

A principal engineer and team builder.

Heri Susanto · Principal Engineer · 7+ yrs

My work sits at the intersection of systems architecture and people. I care as much about the shape of a database query as I do about how the engineer writing it is growing. The best infrastructure, in my experience, is the kind no one has to think about, and the best teams are the ones that make that possible.

I started my career as an Oracle developer, spent time building end-to-end data pipelines for Telkomsel, and shipped fraud prevention systems at Tokopedia (one of Indonesia's largest e-commerce platforms). Today I define technical direction across 39 engineers delivering systems for Indonesian government ministries.

What I'm focused on now: system reliability, engineering culture, mentoring the next wave of technical leaders, and the unglamorous work of making complex infrastructure boring.
